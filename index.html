Overview

Your Gateway to the Heart of the Wild

At Book myDandeli, we believe that everyone deserves a break from the relentless pace of city life. Nestled in the lush, emerald canopy of the Western Ghats, we specialize in crafting immersive nature experiences that reconnect you with the wilderness.

Whether you are looking for a high-octane adventure, a serene riverside retreat, or a cozy homestay tucked away in the forest, we provide a curated selection of the best stays in Dandeli. From luxury resorts to budget-friendly student packages, our goal is to make the beauty of Northern Karnataka accessible to every traveller. With deep local roots and a passion for hospitality, we ensure your journey is more than just a trip—it’s a transformation.
