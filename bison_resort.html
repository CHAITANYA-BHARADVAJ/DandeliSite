Getting Here

How to Reach Dandeli

By Air Flight

The nearest airport is Hubli Airport (HBX), approximately 75 km away. Hubli is well-connected to Bengaluru, Mumbai, and Hyderabad. Taxis and cabs to Dandeli are readily available from the airport.

Approx. 1.5 hr drive from airport
By Rail Train

The closest railway station is Dharwad (DWR), around 60 km from Dandeli. Alnavar Junction, 35 km away, is the nearest smaller halt. Trains from Bengaluru, Goa, and Mumbai pass through both stations.

Approx. 1 hr drive from Dharwad
By Road Drive

Dandeli is 460 km from Bengaluru via NH48 and SH57 — roughly an 8-hour drive. From Pune, it is approximately 390 km (7 hrs). The roads are well-maintained with scenic ghats from Dharwad onwards.

Self-drive or hired cab both work well
By Bus Bus

KSRTC and private operators run overnight buses from Bengaluru, Hubli, and Goa to Dandeli. Buses arrive at the Dandeli town bus stand, from where the resort is a short 5-minute drive.

Overnight buses available from major cities
Nearest town: Dandeli (5 km)  ·  Hubli Airport: 75 km  ·  Dharwad Station: 60 km
